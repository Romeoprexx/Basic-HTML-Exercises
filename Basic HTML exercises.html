Here is the recipe for the vegan chilli

    Ingredients
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 ½ teaspoons smoked paprika
  • 4 cloves garlic, pressed or minced
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 2 medium carrots, chopped
  • 2 cups vegetable broth or water
  • 1 large can (28 ounces) or 2 small cans (15 ounces each) diced tomatoes
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 to 2 teaspoons sherry vinegar or red wine vinegar or lime juice
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • ½ teaspoon salt
    Cooking Instructions
  1. In a large Dutch oven or heavy-bottomed pot over medium heat, warm the olive oil until shimmering.
  2. Add the chopped onion, bell pepper, carrot, celery and ¼ teaspoon of the salt.
  3. Stir to combine and cook, stirring occasionally, until the vegetables are tender and the onion is translucent, about 7-10 minutes.
  4. Add the garlic, chili powder, cumin, smoked paprika and oregano.
  5. Add the chopped cilantro, stir to combine, and then mix in the vinegar.
  6. Divide the mixture into individual bowls and serve with garnishes of your choice.
  7. Enjoy the delicacy!
